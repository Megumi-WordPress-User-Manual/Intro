Change a Theme

1. Click "Appearance" and "Themes".

2. You can check the Theme Details.

3. If you would like to see how the theme will look like on your site, click "Live Preview".

If you are happy with the way it looks, click "Activate".

4. You can also click "Add New Theme".

5. You can "Upload a Theme" or

6. "Search themes".
